Cross-locus candidate rejection decision

Original locus:
chr20:10003468 C>G

Rejected candidates:
L2 chr20:10002468 G>A
L3 chr20:10006496 T>C

Reason:
The candidates are too close to the original locus and reuse much of the same physical nanopore read population.

Observed physical-read overlap:
- Original full 24-read set vs L2 ALT set: 12 shared reads
- Original full 24-read set vs L3 ALT set: 11 shared reads
- L2 ALT set vs L3 ALT set: 13 shared reads

Interpretation:
These loci are not statistically independent cross-locus replicates. They may be retained only as local multi-position robustness candidates within one shared read population.

Decision:
Do not use L2 or L3 for cross-locus generalization claims.

Next requirement:
Select distant loci, preferably on different chromosomes, with negligible or zero physical-read overlap.
